About ARIT General Construction

Founded in Washington, DC, ARIT General Construction LLC is a fully licensed, insured, and highly rated construction firm specializing in high-end residential and commercial projects. With over a decade of experience, we’ve partnered with embassies, developers, and homeowners to bring ambitious visions to life with precision and care.


Our team of skilled engineers, craftsmen, and project managers delivers projects that meet the highest standards of quality and compliance. Whether it’s new construction, renovations, or interior fit-outs, we operate with professionalism, integrity, and a commitment to excellence.


ARIT is proud to hold a BuildZoom score of 94, placing us among the top contractors in the DC area. We believe construction is not just about building structures — it’s about building trust, relationships, and long-term value.
